Mode: default

Info

This is a helpful notebook for Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes - Road To Centurion made using this great resource. It is meant to help you exactly the way, you want it to.

By default, all it does is provide an overview of the manual that has slots for taking relevant notes. If you think it is fair to do so, you can enable some tools in the Preferences menu. Some of them will just highlight your notes, to make certain pieces of information stand out, others will highlight sections of the rules based on your notes and some of them will even tell you exactly what to do.

It is completely up to you and the people you play with where you draw the line.

The tools are colorcoded as follows with the highest enabled tool affecting the overall mode displayed at the top:

Completely harmless

These are things that could realistically be achieved with pen and paper, but they do save some time and effort. They usually just highlight important things in your notes.

Discutable

With these there is still no new information introduced, but they do save a lot of effort. For example by making rules with “only if there is a vowel in the serial number” have a darker color when that is not the case.

Certainly unfair

These tools will even go the extra mile to make the choices for you exactly as the rules describe them and there is probably no excuse for using them in any legitimate game as they will do some basic maths for you and completely break easier modules.

Plain cheats

These are here just for me to flex my programming skills or perhaps to explore some more complex properties of the modules outside of actual games. These things will do all the computation I can come up with to solve some of the more difficult or even chance based puzzles.

This is work in progress, but you can take notes down here :)
